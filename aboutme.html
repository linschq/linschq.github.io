Alina

I'm a policy analyst and data scientist with a keen interest in sustainability within the energy sector. Currently, I work with EITI Indonesia, where I help promote transparency in the extractive industry.

With over four years of experience in data-driven writing and analysis, I strive to create content for various platforms. My background includes a Master of Management degree from Universitas Indonesia, specializing in accounting and finance.

Before this, I worked as a Project Research Analyst at PT Sagatrade Murni, an oil and gas company. There, I developed projects using idle assets and facilitated community-based land acquisition. I also contributed to sustainability initiatives and strategic planning.

I'm passionate about using data and writing to make a positive social and environmental impact. I believe in the power of transparency and data to drive sustainable development and policy change.

Other stuff I do

I write articles and newsletters and have a platform for sharing data nerd content. Additionally, I host creative corners and book club discussions.
